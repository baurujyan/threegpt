What is ThreeGPT?

ThreeGPT is an innovative conversation platform that simulates natural discussions between three AI agents: James, Mike, and Bob. Each agent has a unique role in creating meaningful, focused conversations about any topic you choose.

Meet Our AI Agents

  • James:

    The helpful advisor who provides practical solutions and insights using everyday language in 2-3 concise sentences.

  • Mike:

    The curious questioner who seeks advice and engages in focused discussions while keeping responses brief and relevant.

  • Bob:

    The summary specialist who provides clear, one-sentence summaries of the main solutions discussed.

How It Works

  • Start by entering any topic you'd like to discuss
  • James and Mike will engage in a natural conversation about your topic
  • Get different perspectives and insights on your topic
  • Press ESC at any time to get a summary from Bob
  • Start a new topic whenever you want

Key Features

Natural, human-like conversations
Focused discussions with clear objectives
Automatic message validation
Concise, practical responses
Easy-to-use interface
Real-time conversation summaries

Quality Control

Every message in ThreeGPT goes through our advanced validation system that ensures:

  • Responses are always 2-3 sentences long
  • Language remains natural and casual
  • Messages stay focused on the topic
  • No unnecessary formatting or meta-text
  • Clear and direct communication
